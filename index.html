About Me

I am a rising sophomore at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology. I am on the Varsity basketball team at TJ and am a part of FBLA, ACSL, and PF debate. Outside of school, I play squash, watch Food Insider, read books, and take 45 minute showers.

Basketball

I have been playing AAU basketball for 4 years and am currently on the Varsity basketball team at TJ. During freshman year I was awarded with the rookie of the year award an honorable mention from NOVA 19.

Debate

I started doing public forum debate in 6th grade and have continued to improve every year. In middle school I was a quarterfinalist at nationals and also attended numerous debate camps. Aside from that, I also like to judge middle school debates.

ACSL

In 7th grade I participated in ACSL classroom division and placed second in my school. For 8th and 9th grade I 8th and 9th grade I achieved a perfect score on the written exam and made it to the ACSL all star competition.
